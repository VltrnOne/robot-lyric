How to Play

Player 1 - Safari Explorer:

  • Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Sprint: Hold Shift (uses stamina)
  • Freeze Powers: Press 1 (Red), 2 (Blue), 3 (Green), 4 (Orange)
  • Goal: Collect all pellets while avoiding dinosaurs

Player 2 - Dinosaur Controller:

  • Movement: I/J/K/L keys
  • Switch Dinosaurs: U (Raptor), O (Pterodactyl), P (Triceratops), [ (T-Rex)
  • Special Ability: Space Bar
  • Cycle Dinosaurs: Tab
  • Goal: Catch the safari explorer

System Controls:

  • Pause: ESC key
  • Fullscreen: F11 or button above
  • Mute: Audio button in game footer
